Internal tools for catalog, pricing & listings analysis

We use Amazon Selling Partner API (SP‑API) to retrieve catalog, pricing and listings restriction data for our own store. Data is used solely for operational analytics (ungated checks, profitability, stock planning).

Get in touch

What we build

Internal dashboards to combine Catalog Items, Pricing, Fees and Listings Restrictions endpoints. No third‑party reselling of Amazon data.

Security & Compliance

Least‑privilege access, encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest, credential vaulting, incident response and access reviews — aligned with Amazon’s Acceptable Use and Data Protection policies.

Scopes we request

  • catalog:read — Catalog Items
  • pricing:read — Product pricing & competitive prices
  • listings:read — Listings restrictions

Use case

Cross‑border retail arbitrage (US→CA). We check sellability (gating), compare costs, and maintain compliant listings.
